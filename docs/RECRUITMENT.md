# Therapist Recruitment Kit

Goal: onboard the first **15–25 BACP/UKCP/BPS/NCS-registered therapists**. This is the #1 launch gate — everything else is theoretical without supply.

## The one-line pitch
> Faresay is UK online therapy that pays therapists 85% — not 35%. You set your rates and hours, we bring you clients and handle bookings, payments and video. No subscription traps, no race to the bottom.

## Why a therapist should care (the numbers)
| | BetterHelp | Faresay |
|---|---|---|
| You keep per session | ~35% (~£24) | **85% (~£42–47)** |
| Set your own rate | No | **Yes** |
| Set your own hours | Limited | **Yes** |
| Paid out | Opaque | **2 business days after each session** |
| Client lock-in tactics | Yes | **None** |

Founder note to lead with: *"We're technologists who think no company should profit from human suffering. We take 15% to keep the lights on and pass the rest to you. That's the whole model."*

## Who to target (and where)
- **BACP / UKCP directories** — therapists already advertising privately, looking for more referrals.
- **r/therapists, r/TalkTherapy** (Reddit) — read rules first; post in self-promo/threads only.
- **LinkedIn** — search "BACP registered", "psychotherapist UK", "counsellor private practice". Personalised DMs.
- **Facebook groups** — "UK Counsellors & Psychotherapists", peer-supervision groups.
- **Therapist Slack/Discord communities.**
- **Word of mouth** — once you have 2–3, the in-app **therapist referral** (£25/referral) does the work. Lead with that.

### Referral nudge (to existing therapists)
> You've got a £25 referral link in your dashboard. Know a great colleague who's tired of giving away two-thirds of their fee? Share it — you both win when they run their first session.

## Objection handling
- **"Is it legit / who's behind it?"** → Small UK team, transparent 15% fee, verified therapists only, GDPR-compliant, encrypted video. Point to /about.
- **"What about my insurance/liability?"** → You work as an independent contractor and keep your own PI insurance (standard for the field). Faresay facilitates booking + payment only.
- **"How many clients will I actually get?"** → Honest answer: we're early; founding therapists get first pick of inbound demand and a permanent place at the top of matches. No guarantees, no cost to join.
- **"What's the catch on 85%?"** → None. 15% covers payments, video, software, support. We don't upsell or lock clients in.

## Onboarding the therapist (what they do)
1. Sign up at faresay.com → "I am a therapist".
2. Profile + bio + rate (live percentile shows where they sit) + specialisms + approaches.
3. Enter BACP/UKCP/BPS/NCS registration number → we verify (audit-logged).
4. Stripe Connect Express (real KYC, ~5 min) → enables the 85/15 split.
5. Set availability. Go live once verified (≤2 business days).

## Founding-therapist incentives to consider
- **Reduced fee** for founders (e.g. 10% not 15%) for the first 6–12 months — locks loyalty.
- **"Founding therapist" badge** on profile — trust signal for early clients.
- **£25 peer-referral** already built — make it the main growth loop.

## Track it
Keep a simple sheet: name · body · status (contacted / call booked / onboarding / live) · source. Aim for a 20–30% reply→onboard rate; 60–80 quality outreaches → ~15–20 therapists.
