Faresay logo — a teal disc with the calm "smile" mark and the lowercase Fraunces wordmark.

```jsx
<Logo />                       {/* dark word on light bg */}
<Logo tone="light" />          {/* white, for brand-900 footers/sections */}
<Logo showWord={false} size={36} />  {/* mark only */}
```

Always lowercase "faresay". Use `tone="light"` on dark surfaces. The wordmark scales with `size`.
