# Faresay Practice Portal — Record of Processing Activities (ROPA)

This is Faresay's record under **Article 30 of the UK GDPR**. Faresay holds two roles:

- **As Processor** (Art 30(2)) — for the client/clinical data it processes on behalf of Customers (therapists).
- **As Controller** (Art 30(1)) — for its own account-administration, billing, security and analytics data.

It is a living document, reviewed at least annually and on material change.

## Lawful basis for special-category data

For client health data processed **as Processor**, the lawful basis is determined by the Customer (Controller) — typically Art 9(2)(h) (provision of health/social-care by a professional under a confidentiality duty) and/or Art 9(2)(a) (explicit consent). Faresay does not determine the purposes of the clinical processing.
